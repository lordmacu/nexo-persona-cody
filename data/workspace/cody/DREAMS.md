# Dreams

Auto-generated dream consolidations land here when Cody's
`dreaming.enabled = true` config triggers (default once every
24 h per `cody.yaml::dreaming.interval_secs: 86400`).

A dream consolidation summarises recent transcripts +
project-tracker activity into a short narrative for Cody to
reflect on at the start of the next session. Useful for
operators who run Cody intermittently and want continuity
across days.

The starter is empty — entries appear after the first dream
runs.
